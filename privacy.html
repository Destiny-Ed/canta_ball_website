Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

Welcome to Canta Ball. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile game.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google, Apple, or anonymously, we may collect your name, email, and profile picture.
  • Gameplay Data: Scores, matches played, wins/losses, coins earned, and progress.
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, and unique identifiers for analytics and anti-cheat.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the game (features used, time spent, etc.).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve Canta Ball
  • To personalize your experience and show relevant content
  • To enable online multiplayer and social features
  • To detect and prevent cheating or abuse
  • To send important updates and support messages

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted services:

  • Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Analytics)
  • RevenueCat (In-app purchases)
  • Google Play Games / Game Center (Leaderboards)

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share anonymized data with analytics partners to improve the game.

5. Your Rights

You can request to access, correct, or delete your data by contacting us at cognittoapps@gmail.com.

6. Children's Privacy

Canta Ball is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes.

If you have any questions, please contact us at cognittoapps@gmail.com
